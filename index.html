Peng Luo
Postdoctoral Researcher & Adjunct Assistant Professor
MIT Senseable City Lab & University of Iowa

Peng Luo is a postdoctoral researcher at the MIT Senseable City Lab and an adjunct assistant professor in the School of Earth, Environment, and Sustainability at the University of Iowa. He received his Ph.D. from the Chair of Cartography and Visual Analytics at the Technical University of Munich. His research focuses on Trustworthy GeoAI, with particular interests in geospatial explainable AI, uncertainty quantification for GeoAI models, and ethical spatial analysis pipelines.
