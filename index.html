Peng Luo
Postdoc Research Fellow
Senseable City Lab, Massachusetts Institute of Technology

My research lies at the intersection of GeoAI, spatial data science, and spatial statistics. I develop explainable, uncertainty-aware, and reproducible methods for spatial analysis, with recent work on LLM-driven geospatial model discovery. I received my Ph.D. from the Technical University of Munich and hold an M.S. from Peking University.
