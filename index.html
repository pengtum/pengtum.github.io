Peng Luo
Assistant Professor
School of Spatial Artificial Intelligence, East China Normal University

I work at the intersection of photogrammetry, 3D/2D Gaussian splatting, and thermal infrared sensing, with a focus on building-scale physics-grounded surface reconstruction and thermal anomaly detection. I completed my doctoral work at TUM and am joining ECNU as Assistant Professor in August 2025.
