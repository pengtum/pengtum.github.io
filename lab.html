Openings

We are recruiting PhD students for Fall 2025 / Spring 2026

We are looking for motivated students with a background in one or more of the following:

  • Computer vision, 3D reconstruction, or neural rendering
  • Remote sensing, photogrammetry, or LiDAR processing
  • Thermal infrared sensing or building physics
  • Deep learning with PyTorch or JAX

To apply, please send your CV, transcripts, and a brief research statement to your@email.com. Applications without these materials will not be considered.
